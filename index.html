How we work with you

Jessups has three distinct service lines: Trust and Corporate Services, Accounting and Tax. Our services are tailored to our clients’ needs and we can offer a root to branch service ensuring our clients’ tax and regulatory reporting requirements are maintained. Below are the types of services we offer to our clients.

Trust and Corporate Services

  • Company formation
  • Provision of registered office
  • Company secretarial services
  • Assistance with Group reporting and complex accounting
  • Company directorships
  • Trust formation
  • Acting as Trustee
  • Company and Trust day to day administration including family office
  • Acting as agent for Isle of Man 2006 Act companies

Accounting

  • Bookkeeping
  • Cash management, budget and forecasting
  • Preparation of management accounts and management information
  • Preparation of standalone and group financial statements
  • Assistance with complex accounting
  • Liaising with auditors as applicable
  • VAT and corporate tax compliance
  • Payroll

Tax

  • Isle of Man Tax compliance and advisory
  • End of tax cap planningy
  • Moving you and your business to the Isle of Man
  • UK tax compliance and advisory
  • UK remittance basis planning
  • UK inheritance tax planning
  • Tax disputes and investigations in the UK and the Isle of Man
  • Tax compliance for offshore Trusts and Companies in the UK

Who we are?

Jessups was founded by Brian Jessup in 1977 and moved to its current address on Athol Street in 2001. Built on the core values of listening to clients, investing in long term relationships, delivering exceptional client service and being transparent on fees, the business has prospered and remains family owned and independent to this day. The Experience

Head of Trust and Corporate Services

Wendy Jessup

Prior to moving to Jessups, Wendy worked for PwC in both the UK and the Isle of Man for 15 years, specialising in audit and accounting advisory. Wendy has advised global clients on a wide range of UK GAAP, IFRS and US GAAP accounting issues. Wendy is ACA qualified and is a student member of the Institute of Directors.

Ben Holloway

Following a legal career, Ben began his accounting career at KPMG in the Isle of Man before moving to PwC in both the Isle of Man and UK over a 15 year ‘Big Four’ Career. Prior to moving to Jessups, Ben was Head of a leading international city law firm’s Private Wealth department. Ben is CTA qualified and a member of the bar of England and Wales (non-practicing).

Arthur Curran

Arthur worked with Brian from 1977 and brings a wealth of knowledge and experience of dealing with clients ranging from complex multi-jurisdictional Family Offices to individuals seeking help with their accounting and personal tax compliance.
